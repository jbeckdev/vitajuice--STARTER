Our Story

VitaJuice began as a small company in Eastern California, selling smoothies to surfers after a long day in the sun. We quickly grew to franchise status, and have now set up shop around beaches and paradises across the globe. We offer smoothies with amazing taste, healthy ingredients, and a good dash of protein, all for an affordable price. Have one delivered to you right away!
